Located in Lagos, Nigeria, Erruki Gulf Club offers a premier golfing experience amidst serene landscapes. With an 18-hole course and cutting-edge practice facilities, the club caters to golfers of all levels. More than golf, Erruki Gulf Club fosters a community centered on sportsmanship and camaraderie, providing an inclusive environment with top-notch amenities in its clubhouse. Founded on excellence and hospitality, the club invites individuals and families to embrace a lifestyle enriched by golf and community spirit.
